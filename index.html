About PEACE-SALONE

People's Effort Advocating for Community Empowerment Sierra Leone (PEACE-SALONE) is a Local Non-Governmental Organization in Sierra Leone, that was established on 13 April 2014 by Sierra Leonean Aruna Kamara.

We are committed to contributing to national development through civic empowerment programs to promote greater and active participation in decision making and raising the voices of citizens in the democratic governance of Sierra Leone.

Our work is guided by global organizational strategies and Sierra Leone's Mid Term National Development Plan with specific thematic strategies aligned with national development priorities.

Our Vision

PEACE-SALONE exists to have a society with people that are well empowered with ideas or knowledge to make informed decision to improve their lives.

Our Mission

PEACE-SALONE exists to guard democracy, campaigner of peace, good governance and socio-economic development of Sierra Leone.

Our Goal & Key Objectives

Goal

Holistic Nation Building

To contribute to nation building through a holistic, integrated development approach.

1

Equitable Healthcare for All

To advocate for a resilient and responsive healthcare system that ensures equitable, affordable, and quality services for all Sierra Leoneans across all life stages.

2

Empowering Women and Youth

To promote investment and opportunities for women, youth, children, and persons with disabilities in political, social, economic, and cultural spheres.

3

Healthy Environment, Healthy People

To protect the environment to improve access to safe, diverse, and nutritious food, reducing malnutrition in women and children.

4

Good Governance & Accountability

To foster a just, inclusive, and law-governed society with protected rights and responsible financial management.

5

Advancing Research & Knowledge

To expand understanding of critical issues through scientific research and evidence-based methods.

Our 5-Year Strategic Plan

Building a Sustainable Future for Sierra Leone

Our comprehensive 5-year strategic plan outlines our roadmap for creating lasting impact in Sierra Leone through targeted programs, capacity building, and community engagement. The plan focuses on sustainable development across all our thematic areas with measurable outcomes.

Vision 2025-2030

To become a leading force in community empowerment and sustainable development in Sierra Leone, creating measurable impact across all districts.

Strategic Goals

  • Expand operations to 3 additional districts
  • Impact over 50,000 lives directly through our programs
  • Establish partnerships with 10 international organizations
  • Develop a sustainable funding model

Key Focus Areas

  • Program expansion and diversification
  • Organizational capacity building
  • Community engagement and ownership
  • Research and evidence-based programming
  • Advocacy and policy influence
